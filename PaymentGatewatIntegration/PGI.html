ABOUT US

Sparks Foundation (Charitables) is actively involved and engaged in the climate negotiations that take place under the aegis of United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC), it has recently been accredited as an Observer organization to the UNFCCC process at the 19th Conference of Parties held at Warsaw, Poland. The organization is also involved in following other multi-lateral forums such as the G-8, G-20, and the Green Climate Fund to name a few.
